Description

In a far, far future, a far more advanced alien race has declared war
against humans and is now trying to destroy Earth by sending an asteroid rain.
You, as a brave soldier along with others, are on a mission to clear the sky of these asteroids.
The most advanced attack ship is placed in your experienced hands.
You can choose from 8 different ship models

that can carry 3 types of energy weapons (each 3 fire modes), or 2 types of burst guns and rockets

Highlights

- Great graphics
- 2D Particles
- Random Generated Asteroids
- 8 ships
- 6 weapon types( 3 energy weapons, 2 burst guns, 1 rocket type)

Technical Highlights

- Direct Draw
- Alpha Blending
- 2D Particle engine
- FMOD for sound/music
