Description

2AM combined the sweetness of Hello Kitty character with the agility and logic of a tetris game and created for all of you, Hello Kitty fans, a perfect game to waste your time and have some fun with not so much headache. :-)
When you are done playing alone you can invite a friend to join you in a knife edge tetris game of who survives longer with the other player making trouble for you all the time. ;-)
This game is dedicated to all Hello Kitty fans.

For 2AM is a tradition to release a new version each Christmas. Stay close to quickly get this year version 3 of the game that will include INTERNET 2 players game and many other cool features.

You look worried :-P, dont be,you can play too, we took care hello kitty 2 can run perfectly on very slow computers.

Highlights

- Cool graphics with perfect colors to warm your soul
- 1 player, 2 players games
- 10 levels with incresed difficulty

Technical Highlights

- freeform windows, hover buttons
