Description

Yet another visualization plugin for Winamp 2.x version.
Basicly some mumbo-jumbo of colors based on the data from the song.

Highlights

- 4 presets
- unresizeable window mode only :-)

Technical Highlights

- MMX assembler instructions for better speed
- other efects can be created very easy
- blur and darken efect
- doesnt require DirectX

For additional information and SUGGESTIONS contact one of the team members.
